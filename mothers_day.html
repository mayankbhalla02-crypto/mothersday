Dear Maa,

There are not enough words in any language to fully describe what you mean to me. But today, on this special day, I want to try — because you deserve to hear it, even when I forget to say it often enough.

You have been my safe place in every storm, the first voice I want to hear when something goes wrong, and the first person I want to call when something goes right. You have given me more than I could ever repay — not just in things, but in the quiet, steady love that shaped everything I am.

I see it now, more than I ever did before — the sacrifices you made without ever calling them sacrifices. The late nights, the worries you carried alone so I wouldn't have to, the way you always put everyone else first and made it look effortless.

You are the reason I believe in kindness, in patience, in showing up for the people you love no matter what. Every good thing in me came from watching you.

Thank you, Maa. For everything — for every single thing. I love you more than this page could ever hold.

Forever and always, your child — Maynk and pari
